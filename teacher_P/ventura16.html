About Ventura

Ventura is the sports event of IIIT Vadodara to bring out the talent of the students
Itcomprises of many sports events such as football,volleyball,cricket,box-cricket,chess,tug of war,..etc.
Year 2016 witnessed the second edition of the event ,decorated by awesome performance by the participants.
