About XERIC

The first ever cultural fest of IIITV - 'Xeric 2k15' started off with a bang on the evening of 3rd April. The fest continued for two days with the first day being reserved for various performances put up by the different clubs of the college while the highlights of the second day were the mystery event Obscura and the treasure hunt. The events of the first day started with 'Sashay down the red carpet' where all the participants walked down the ramp in their best attire and attitude. This was followed by a Qawwali performance which had the audience in splits. Three dramas were performed each focusing on different themes which empowerment to the plight of middle class families. The first day concluded with the most awaited dance drama performance by the dance club members recounting the journey of the students in the college and captured the nuances of our college life.
